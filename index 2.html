Kobo cover helper

The Laurie Fix

For readers who believe an organized library should also be a pretty one. This adds the real cover page Kobo expects after a cover has been added to an AO3 EPUB.

Three steps. No snakes.

  1. Add the cover first. Open Alex Chan’s Cover Tool
  2. Choose the covered EPUB here. You may select several at once.
  3. Download the new files ending in Kobo Ready, then place them in your Rakuten Kobo folder.
